Smart LPG Regulator

IoT & Safety

Project Summary

Designed, developed, and implemented an intelligent, IoT-enabled **Smart LPG Regulator** to actively mitigate domestic leakages and track fuel consumption in real-time.

Academic Credentials

Minor Project-II (3.2 Semester)
Submitted in partial fulfillment of the award of B.Tech. in Information Technology at Vel Tech Rangarajan Dr. Sagunthala R&D Institute of Science and Technology, May 2026.
Team: Raju Praveen Kumar Reddy (23UEIT0045), G Sasidhar Sai Varma (23UEIT0013), G Dhanush (23UEIT0012).
Guidance: Dr. Lijetha C Jaffrin, Assistant Professor (SG).

Technical Specifications

Microcontroller ESP32 DevKit V1 (Wi-Fi-enabled dual-core processing).
Sensors Interfaced MQ-2 combustible gas sensor & precision load cell with HX711 24-bit ADC.
Actuation Logic ESP32 commands relay to cut off a heavy-duty 12V Solenoid Valve in <1 second.
Telemetry & Override Blynk IoT Cloud Platform integrating real-time telemetry and push alerts.

Core Features

  • Autonomous Fail-Safe: Instantly de-energizes the solenoid valve to sever gas flow when ambient leak levels exceed the 3500 ADC threshold.
  • Real-Time Cloud Telemetry: Feeds fuel levels, remaining percentages, and safety indicators directly to a Blynk dashboard.
  • Manual Valve Actuation: Enables remote closing/opening of the gas valve through user smartphone control.
